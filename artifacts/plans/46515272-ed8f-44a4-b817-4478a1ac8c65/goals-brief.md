## Business Objectives
The core objective for **HELLO** is to transition from a boutique design firm to a recognized leader in high-end brand strategy. The business aims to increase its average contract value by 40% over the next 12 months by attracting enterprise-level partnerships and tech-sector innovators.

## Website Goals
*   **Primary:** Convert high-intent visitors into qualified leads through a strategic "Discovery Call" funnel.
*   **Secondary:** Serve as a premium portfolio hub that demonstrates ROI-driven creative work and thought leadership.
*   **User Experience:** Establish an immediate "wow factor" through interactive storytelling that reflects the agency’s technical prowess.

## Target Audience
*   **Series B Founders:** Tech leaders seeking a sophisticated brand evolution to support international scaling.
*   **Marketing Directors:** Corporate decision-makers at mid-market firms looking for a reliable, creative external partner.
*   **Top-Tier Talent:** Creative professionals evaluating the agency's culture and portfolio for potential employment.

## Success Metrics (KPIs)
*   **Inquiry Quality:** A 25% increase in lead qualification scores based on budget and project scope.
*   **Engagement:** Achieve an average session duration of >2.5 minutes on the "Case Studies" section.
*   **Conversion:** Maintain a minimum 4% click-through rate (CTR) on the primary Call-to-Action (CTA).

## Project Constraints
*   **Timeline:** Fixed 10-week development window to align with the upcoming industry keynote.
*   **Performance:** Must maintain a Google Lighthouse score of 90+ for mobile performance despite heavy video content.
*   **Platform:** Development is restricted to Webflow for ease of content management by the internal marketing team.
