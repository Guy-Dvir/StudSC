## Brand Personality
The personality of **HELLO** is high-energy, radically approachable, and human-centric. It is designed to feel like a warm handshake—an invitation to connect rather than a corporate barrier. The brand prioritizes clarity over cleverness, positioning itself as an essential, friendly tool for modern communication.

## Tone of Voice
*   **Welcoming & Optimistic:** Every touchpoint should feel encouraging and bright.
*   **Direct:** Jargon-free language that respects the user's time.
*   **Punchy:** Use of short sentences and active verbs to maintain momentum.

## Color Palette
*   **Solar Yellow (#FFD700):** The primary brand color, used for high-impact accents to evoke warmth and energy.
*   **Midnight Navy (#0F172A):** The foundation color for typography and dark-mode elements, providing a sense of stability and trust.
*   **Electric Indigo (#6366F1):** Used for primary Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons and interactive states to drive engagement.
*   **Soft Smoke (#F8FAFC):** A clean, off-white background shade to reduce eye strain and provide visual breathing room.

## Typography Direction
*   **Headlines:** **Montserrat (Bold)**. A geometric sans-serif that is both modern and incredibly legible at large scales.
*   **Body Text:** **Inter**. A highly functional typeface optimized for screen readability, offering a neutral yet friendly character.

## Visual Style References
The aesthetic follows a **High-Contrast Minimalist** direction with "Pop-Art" sensibilities. This includes oversized typography, generous white space, and "squircle" (rounded square) interface components. Visuals should feature candid, high-quality photography of diverse people combined with vibrant, 2D vector illustrations to keep the brand feeling tactile and alive.
