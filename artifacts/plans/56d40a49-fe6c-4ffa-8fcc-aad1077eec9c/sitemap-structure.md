## Site Hierarchy & Sitemap

*   **Home** (High-energy hero video, real-time availability, and "How it Works")
*   **The Experience**
    *   **Attractions** (Individual landing pages for Arcade, VR, and Bowling)
    *   **The FUNN Guide** (Rules, safety, and player requirements)
*   **Eat & Drink**
    *   **Full Menu** (Mobile-optimized digital menu)
    *   **Bar & Cocktails** (Happy hour and signature drinks)
*   **Parties & Events**
    *   **Corporate Team Building** (Work packages)
    *   **Birthdays & Celebrations** (Youth and adult packages)
    *   **Full Venue Buyouts** (Large-scale event logistics)
*   **Locations** (Dynamic store locator with unique hours/amenities)
*   **FUNN Club** (Loyalty program login and rewards dashboard)
*   **Contact** (General inquiries and feedback)

## Main Navigation Structure

*   **Primary Nav:** Activities, Eat & Drink, Parties, Locations.
*   **Utility Nav:** Member Login, Gift Cards, Cart.
*   **Global CTA:** [Book Now] (High-contrast button persistent across all viewports).

## Key User Flows

1.  **The Quick Player:** Home → Select Location → Browse Activities → Real-time Calendar → Direct Booking/Checkout.
2.  **The Event Planner:** Parties Landing → Select Package (e.g., Corporate) → Inquiry Form → Automated Confirmation/Brochure PDF.
3.  **The On-Site Guest:** QR Code Scan → Digital Menu → Mobile Ordering or Rewards Login.

## Page-Level Strategic Notes

*   **Location Landing Pages:** Must include Google Map integration and "Live Wait Times" API for walk-ins.
*   **Activity Pages:** Feature-heavy content with high-resolution video clips to reduce "pre-visit anxiety."
*   **Mobile Experience:** The "Book Now" CTA remains sticky at the bottom of the screen to drive conversion during mobile browsing.
