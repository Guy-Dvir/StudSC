## Business Objectives
The core mission of **FUNN** is to disrupt the stagnant local entertainment market by gamifying social discovery. We aim to bridge the gap between digital interaction and real-world experiences, positioning FUNN as the primary ecosystem for "non-boring" weekend activities. Our commercial objective is to secure a **15% market share** among urban millennial social seekers within the first year through tiered subscription models and event partnerships.

## Website Goals
*   **Primary Goal:** Drive high-intent user conversion through a seamless, mobile-optimized event booking and ticket purchasing funnel.
*   **Secondary Goal:** Increase brand virality by incentivizing "Proof of Fun"—user-generated content and social sharing of attended events to build platform trust.

## Target Audience
*   **The Social Explorer:** Young professionals (Ages 22–35) who feel "app fatigue" and are looking for curated, high-energy, Instagram-worthy experiences.
*   **The Experience Architect:** Local boutique businesses and independent event organizers who need a high-aesthetic platform to reach a niche, active audience.

## Success Metrics (KPIs)
*   **Booking Conversion Rate:** Target a **5% minimum** conversion from landing page to completed checkout.
*   **User Retention:** Achieve a **40% return rate** of users visiting the platform weekly.
*   **Viral Coefficient:** Measure success by an average of **1.5 invites sent** per registered user.

## Project Constraints
*   **Mobile-First Design:** The site must prioritize mobile performance, as **90% of traffic** is expected from social media referrals.
*   **Timeline:** The MVP must be live within **10 weeks** to capitalize on the upcoming summer festival season.
*   **Budget:** Initial development is capped at **$50,000**, requiring a focus on core features over peripheral animations.
