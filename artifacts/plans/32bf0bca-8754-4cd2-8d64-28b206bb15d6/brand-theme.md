## Color Palette
*   **#0B0C10 (Deep Obsidian):** A grounded, premium base that provides high-contrast depth for a luxury tech feel.
*   **#F1F4F8 (Arctic Mist):** A crisp, breathable neutral used for primary backgrounds to maintain a feeling of openness.
*   **#0077B6 (Glacier Blue):** The primary brand color, signaling professional-grade cooling and reliability.
*   **#90E0EF (Frost Spark):** A bright accent used for call-to-action buttons and interactive states.
*   **#FFFFFF (Absolute Zero):** Pure white for high-readability text and minimalist negative space.

## Typography Direction
*   **Primary Typeface:** A geometric sans-serif (e.g., *Inter* or *Montserrat*) used in bold weights for headlines. Wide letter-spacing will be applied to evoke a sense of airiness and modern precision.
*   **Secondary Typeface:** A clean monospaced font (e.g., *JetBrains Mono*) for technical specifications and data points to emphasize the brand's "Smart Tech" DNA.

## Brand Personality
*   **Sophisticated:** Elevated and premium, catering to high-end architectural and residential integration.
*   **Effortless:** Prioritizing a seamless user experience and "invisible" climate technology.
*   **Refreshing:** A modern, disruptive take on a legacy industry, stripping away the clutter of traditional hardware design.

## Tone of Voice
*   **Concise:** Short, punchy copy that values the user's time and gets straight to the benefit.
*   **Authoritative:** Confident in technical performance and sustainability metrics without relying on heavy jargon.
*   **Calming:** Using reassuring language that emphasizes comfort, quietude, and control.

## Visual Style References
*   **Brutalist Minimalism:** Large, high-impact headlines paired with vast amounts of negative space.
*   **Macro Photography:** High-gloss close-ups of brushed metal surfaces, condensation droplets, and sleek digital interfaces.
*   **Fluid Motion:** Subtle CSS transitions and scroll-triggered animations that mimic the natural flow of air.
