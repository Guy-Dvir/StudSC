## Business Objectives
The primary objective for **Cool** is to establish a dominant digital presence within the premium, sustainable streetwear market. We aim to transition from a social-media-reliant sales model to a high-converting, direct-to-consumer (DTC) powerhouse that emphasizes minimalist aesthetics and ethical manufacturing transparency.

## Primary & Secondary Website Goals
*   **Primary Goal:** Drive direct e-commerce conversions through a frictionless, mobile-first checkout experience, specifically optimized for high-demand "product drops."
*   **Secondary Goal:** Build long-term brand equity by capturing first-party data via "Early Access" SMS/email signups and hosting interactive "lookbooks" that reduce return rates through better fit visualization.

## Target Audience Breakdown
*   **The Trendsetter (Ages 18-30):** Design-conscious urban dwellers seeking exclusive, minimalist pieces that signal status and environmental awareness.
*   **The Conscious Professional (Ages 28-45):** High-income earners looking for durable, versatile capsule wardrobe items produced under fair-labor conditions.

## Success Metrics & KPIs
*   **Conversion Rate:** Target a minimum 3.2% conversion rate for organic and social traffic.
*   **Average Order Value (AOV):** Maintain an AOV of $145+ through strategic cross-selling of accessory lines.
*   **Retention:** Achieve a 20% returning customer rate within the first six months.

## Project Constraints
*   **Technical:** Seamless integration with Shopify Plus and existing third-party logistics (3PL) is non-negotiable.
*   **Performance:** Strict adherence to Core Web Vitals; the heavy use of high-resolution video assets must not compromise mobile load speeds.
*   **Timeline:** Total deployment must conclude within an 8-week sprint to align with the "Winter Capsule" launch.
