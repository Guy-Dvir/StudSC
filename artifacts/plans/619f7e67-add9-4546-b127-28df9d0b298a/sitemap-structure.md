## Site Architecture

The structure for **Hi** focuses on a "Mobile-First, Connection-Fast" philosophy, prioritizing ease of access and immediate utility.

*   **Primary Navigation:** Product, Solutions, Pricing, Log In, **Get Started (CTA)**.
*   **Footer:** Legal (Privacy/Terms), Support/FAQ, API Documentation, Social Links.
*   **Page Hierarchy:**
    *   **Home:** High-impact hero section with a "Create Your Link" instant-input field; social proof carousel.
    *   **Platform:** 
        *   **Digital Identity:** Showcase of customizable bio-pages and NFC integration.
        *   **Smart Networking:** Overview of the mini-CRM and lead capture tools.
    *   **Pricing:** Comparative grid featuring Free, Pro, and Enterprise tiers.
    *   **Resources:** 
        *   **The Connect Blog:** Thought leadership on professional networking.
        *   **Help Center:** Searchable database for hardware/software troubleshooting.
    *   **User Dashboard (Auth Required):**
        *   **Profile Builder:** Drag-and-drop interface for card design.
        *   **Analytics:** Real-time tracking of scans, clicks, and geographic engagement.

## Strategic User Flows

*   **The Onboarding Sprint:** Landing Page → Instant Username Reserve → Template Selection → Account Creation → Profile Live.
*   **The Real-World Exchange:** Physical NFC Tap/QR Scan → Mobile-Optimized Profile Page → "Save Contact" Action → Automated "Nice to meet you" Email Trigger.

## Page-Level Logic

*   **Home:** Must prioritize a <2-second load time and a singular, friction-free conversion point.
*   **Profile Pages:** Use aggressive caching and lightweight assets to ensure 100% reliability during live networking events with spotty connectivity.
*   **Analytics:** Visual-heavy data representation focused on "ROI of Connection" to drive Pro-tier retention.
