## Business Objectives
The primary objective for **HFE (Health & Fitness Education)** is to solidify its position as a leading provider of premier fitness and Pilates qualifications. The business seeks to scale annual enrollments by 20% by streamlining the transition from initial inquiry to paid student, while reducing administrative overhead through automated lead nurturing.

## Website Goals
*   **Primary:** Drive high-intent conversions for Level 2, 3, and 4 fitness certifications via integrated booking and payment gateways.
*   **Secondary:** Build long-term brand equity through an SEO-optimized "Fitness Career Hub" and provide a frictionless entry point for current students to access the Learning Management System (LMS).

## Target Audience
*   **Career Changers:** Individuals aged 25–45 seeking a transition into the wellness industry; they prioritize accreditation, flexibility, and post-course support.
*   **Fitness Professionals:** Existing trainers looking for CPD (Continuing Professional Development) or specialized Level 4 qualifications to increase their earning potential.
*   **Corporate Gym Chains:** HR and training managers seeking a reliable partner for large-scale staff upskilling.

## Success Metrics (KPIs)
*   **Conversion Rate:** The percentage of visitors completing a course booking or deposit payment.
*   **Lead Quality:** Monthly volume of "Prospectus Downloads" and "Callback Requests" from qualified candidates.
*   **Search Visibility:** Top 3 rankings for high-intent keywords like "Personal Trainer Course" and "Pilates Teacher Training."

## Project Constraints
*   **Technical Integration:** The site must maintain seamless API connectivity with the existing **Salesforce CRM** and the **Moodle LMS**.
*   **Timeline:** The new site must be fully operational before the January "New Year, New Career" marketing peak.
*   **Compliance:** All content must adhere strictly to awarding body guidelines and OFSTED educational standards.
