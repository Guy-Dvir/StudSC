## Site Hierarchy
*   **Home:** High-impact value proposition focusing on HFE's "Human-Centered Engineering" methodology.
*   **Expertise** (Main Nav)
    *   **User Research:** Contextual inquiry, task analysis, and workflow modeling.
    *   **Product Design:** Industrial design, ergonomics, and rapid prototyping.
    *   **Usability Validation:** Summative testing and regulatory (FDA/ISO) documentation.
*   **Sectors** (Main Nav)
    *   **Medical Devices:** Focus on safety-critical systems and IEC 62366 compliance.
    *   **Aerospace & Defense:** High-stakes human-system integration (HSI) and cockpit design.
    *   **Consumer Tech:** Optimizing user satisfaction and physical interaction ergonomics.
*   **Case Studies:** Categorized by sector with measurable "Before/After" performance metrics.
*   **Knowledge Hub:** Technical blog, whitepapers, and regulatory update alerts.
*   **Company:** History, core values, and consultant credentials/certifications.
*   **Contact:** Multi-step lead qualification form for project scoping.

## Navigation Structure
*   **Primary Nav:** Expertise (Mega-menu), Sectors (Mega-menu), Case Studies, Knowledge Hub, About.
*   **Utility Nav:** Client Portal Login, Search icon.
*   **Header CTA:** "Consult an Expert."
*   **Footer:** Newsletter sign-up, ISO certification badges, Privacy/Legal, and social links.

## Key User Flows
*   **The Compliance Flow:** Medical Manufacturer → Home → Medical Sector Page → Usability Validation Service → Relevant Case Study → Contact (Request for Proposal).
*   **The Innovation Flow:** Product Designer → Search/Social → Whitepaper Landing Page → Expertise: Product Design → About Us (Team Credentials) → Contact.

## Page-Level Notes
*   **Expertise Pages:** Must include "Methodology Callouts" to explain the scientific rigor behind HFE processes.
*   **Case Studies:** Use a structured "Challenge, Action, Result" format with gated "Full Technical Report" downloads to capture high-intent leads.
*   **Sector Pages:** Feature dynamic regulatory badges (e.g., AAMI, NIST) to establish immediate technical authority.
