## Business Objectives & Website Goals
The primary objective for **HELLO** is to establish a premium digital presence that mirrors the high-end, collaborative nature of our physical coworking spaces. We aim to position the brand as the "third space" of choice for local innovators.
*   **Primary Goal:** Drive lead generation by converting site visitors into scheduled physical tours and "Day Pass" purchases.
*   **Secondary Goal:** Reduce administrative overhead by integrating a self-service member portal for room bookings and event registrations.

## Target Audience Breakdown
*   **The Independent Professional:** Freelancers and consultants aged 25–45 seeking a polished environment beyond the home office.
*   **Scaling Tech Startups:** Small teams (3–12 people) requiring flexible private office suites with enterprise-grade infrastructure.
*   **Remote Corporate Teams:** Decentralized departments looking for professional meeting hubs and monthly "anchor" desks.

## Success Metrics & KPIs
*   **Conversion Rate:** Achieve a 4.5% conversion rate on the "Book a Tour" call-to-action within the first six months.
*   **Local SEO:** Rank in the top three results for "coworking near me" and "private office [City Name]" keywords.
*   **Engagement:** Maintain an average session duration of >2:30 minutes on the amenities and floor-plan pages.

## Project Constraints
*   **Technical Integration:** The site must provide real-time availability via a two-way sync with the **Nexudus** membership management API.
*   **Timeline:** Deployment must be finalized within an **8-week sprint** to align with the flagship location's grand opening.
*   **Brand Guidelines:** Strict adherence to the minimalist, high-contrast visual identity, prioritizing mobile-first accessibility.
