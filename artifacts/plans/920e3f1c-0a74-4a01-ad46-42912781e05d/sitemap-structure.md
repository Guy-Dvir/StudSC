## Navigation Architecture
The primary navigation utilizes a "Fixed Minimalist" approach to prioritize visual storytelling and ease of movement.

*   **Main Navigation (Header)**
    *   **Work:** Filterable grid of creative case studies.
        *   *Project Detail:* Deep-dive pages with video headers and results-driven metrics.
    *   **Services:** Overview of core competencies.
        *   Brand Strategy & Identity
        *   Digital Product Design
        *   Content Production
    *   **About:** Mission statement, core team bios, and "The Hello Method."
    *   **Contact:** Direct inquiry portal and global office locations.
*   **Utility Navigation (Footer)**
    *   Careers, Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Social Media (Instagram, LinkedIn, Behance).

## Key User Flows
*   **The Conversion Path:** Home → Work (Case Study) → Service Alignment → Contact Form. Designed for high-intent prospects seeking social proof before reaching out.
*   **The Expertise Path:** Home → Services → About (The Method) → Contact. Focused on clients looking for specific strategic methodologies and cultural fit.
*   **The Talent Path:** Home → About (Team/Culture) → Careers → Application. Optimized for employer branding and recruitment.

## Page-Level Strategy & Notes
*   **Home Page:** Features a full-screen cinematic reel to establish brand "vibe" immediately. Uses a modular "Drag-and-Drop" layout for easy content updates.
*   **Case Studies:** Employs a **Success Metric Sidebar** to highlight ROI/results alongside creative assets.
*   **Contact Page:** Includes a **Dynamic Lead Qualifier** (budget/timeline dropdowns) to streamline the sales pipeline.
*   **Global Elements:** Implementation of a **Persistent CTA** in the header to ensure "Start a Project" is never more than one click away.
