## Business Objectives
The primary objective for **Hi** is to disrupt the saturated digital networking market by providing the world’s most minimalist, frictionless contact-sharing interface. We aim to capture 50,000 active users within the first two quarters and establish **Hi** as the premier digital alternative to traditional paper business cards for the modern professional.

## Primary & Secondary Website Goals
*   **Primary:** Drive immediate user acquisition through a "one-click" account creation and card setup process.
*   **Secondary:** Showcase the platform’s aesthetic versatility through a live "template previewer" and provide a resource hub for networking etiquette in a digital-first world.

## Target Audience Breakdown
*   **The Creative Freelancer:** Value-driven professionals (designers, photographers) who prioritize aesthetic integrity and ease of portfolio sharing.
*   **Enterprise Sales Teams:** High-volume networkers requiring unified brand identity and centralized lead management.
*   **Tech-Savvy Minimalists:** Early adopters who favor "less but better" tools and seek to reduce their physical footprint.

## Success Metrics & KPIs
*   **Conversion Rate:** Target a 12–15% conversion rate from landing page visit to account activation.
*   **Viral Coefficient:** Measure the ratio of new sign-ups originating from a shared "Hi" link.
*   **Performance:** Achieve a sub-1.5 second load time to reinforce the brand promise of speed and efficiency.

## Project Constraints
*   **Technical:** The platform must maintain 100% functionality on legacy mobile browsers.
*   **Design:** Strict adherence to a monochrome, typography-heavy aesthetic; zero use of stock photography.
*   **Timeline:** An MVP (Minimum Viable Product) launch is required within a 6-week development window.
