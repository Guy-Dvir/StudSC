## Visual Identity & Brand Essence
**Brand Personality:** The brand, tentatively named 'Aetheria Wellness & Spa,' embodies 'Serene Sophistication.' It is a sanctuary of calm that balances clinical expertise with holistic luxury. The brand should feel breathable, organic, and premium.

**Color Palette:**
-   **Deep Moss (#3A4D39):** Used for primary headings and grounding elements to evoke nature and stability.
-   **Soft Sage (#8DAA91):** Secondary accents and icons, representing healing and growth.
-   **Warm Alabaster (#F9F7F2):** The primary background color to avoid the harshness of pure white and provide a paper-like, tactile feel.
-   **Muted Gold (#C5A059):** Reserved for call-to-action buttons and luxury highlights (e.g., 'Book Now').

**Typography Direction:**
-   **Primary Headings:** *Cormorant Garamond* (Serif). Elegant, timeless, and high-end.
-   **Secondary/Body:** *Montserrat* or *Lato* (Sans-serif). Clean, highly legible, and modern for service descriptions and forms.

**Tone of Voice:** The voice is 'Whispered Authority.' It is calming and empathetic but clearly knowledgeable. We use sensory-rich language (e.g., 'unwind,' 'rejuvenate,' 'luminous') while maintaining a professional medical-grade clarity for skin treatments.

**Visual References:** Minimalist layouts, high use of negative space, and macro photography of botanical ingredients or water ripples.
