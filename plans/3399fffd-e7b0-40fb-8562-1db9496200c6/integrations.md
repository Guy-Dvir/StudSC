## Technical Ecosystem

**Essential Integrations (Phase 1):**
*   **Booking & Scheduling:** *Mindbody* or *Acuity Scheduling*. This must be a deep integration allowing users to see real-time availability without leaving the site.
*   **Payment Gateway:** *Stripe* for secure credit card processing, including Apple Pay and Google Pay for mobile users.
*   **Email Marketing:** *Klaviyo* or *Mailchimp*. Automated flows for 'Welcome,' 'Birthday Rewards,' and 'Re-booking Reminders' (30 days post-treatment).
*   **Analytics:** *Google Analytics 4 (GA4)* with enhanced ecommerce tracking for gift card sales.

**Nice-to-Have Integrations (Phase 2):**
*   **Live Chat/SMS:** *Podium* or *Intercom* for instant guest communication and lead capture.
*   **Social Proof:** *Instagram Feed (SnapWidget)* to display real-time 'Sanctuary Life' photos.
*   **Reviews:** *Yelp/Google Review* widget to pull in the latest 5-star testimonials automatically.
*   **Loyalty Portal:** A dedicated login area for members to view points and past treatments.

**Security & Compliance:** SSL certification is mandatory. The site will avoid collecting sensitive HIPAA-related medical data directly, instead routing medical intake forms through the secure booking engine.
