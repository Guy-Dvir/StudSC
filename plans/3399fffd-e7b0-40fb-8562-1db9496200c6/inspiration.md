## Design Direction & Aesthetics

**Visual Mood:** The aesthetic is 'Modern Zen.' We are moving away from the 'purple-lit clinical' look and toward a 'warm, minimalist boutique' feel. The design should utilize 'Negative Space' to give the user's eyes a place to rest, mirroring the spa experience itself.

**3-5 Specific Aesthetic References:**
1.  **Aire Ancient Baths:** For their masterful use of dark, moody imagery and focus on 'the experience' over technical jargon.
2.  **Aman Resorts:** For their elite minimalism, grid-based layouts, and focus on architectural beauty.
3.  **Bamford Wellness:** For their 'earthy' and 'organic' digital presence that feels both high-end and sustainable.
4.  **The Now Massage:** For their modern, accessible, and highly mobile-optimized booking flow.

**What to Avoid:**
*   **Generic Stock Photos:** No 'woman with a flower behind her ear' or 'stacked river stones'—these feel dated and cheap.
*   **Cluttered Sidebars:** We want a single-column focus to maintain a sense of calm.
*   **Aggressive Pop-ups:** Any lead capture should be subtle (e.g., a slide-in or footer-bound offer) to avoid disrupting the peaceful browsing experience.
