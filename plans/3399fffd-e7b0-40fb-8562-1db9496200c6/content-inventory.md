## Required Content Assets

**Copywriting Requirements:**
*   **Service Menu:** SEO-optimized descriptions for 20+ treatments, focusing on benefits rather than just features.
*   **Team Bios:** 5-7 professional bios highlighting certifications and 'specialty techniques.'
*   **FAQ Section:** Addressing arrival times, cancellation policies, and what to wear.
*   **Blog Content:** 4 initial 'pillar' articles regarding skincare routines and wellness trends.

**Photography & Video:**
*   **Hero Video:** 15-second ambient loop of the spa interior, steam, and a treatment in progress (slow motion).
*   **Interior Photography:** 10 high-resolution shots of treatment rooms, the lounge area, and the reception.
*   **Action Shots:** Close-ups of hands-on treatments (e.g., stones on a back, facial mask application) featuring diverse models.
*   **Product Shots:** High-quality 'lifestyle' photography of retail products for the shop section.

**Existing Assets to Collect:**
*   Current logo files (Vector format).
*   Price list in CSV/PDF format.
*   Staff certification documents for 'About' page verification.
*   Current client database for the initial email launch campaign.
