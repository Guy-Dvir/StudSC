## Site Architecture & User Flow

**Main Navigation:**
*   **Home:** Hero video, value proposition, featured services, and trust signals.
*   **Services:** Categorized dropdown (Facials, Massages, Body Rituals, Med-Spa).
*   **Packages:** Curated half-day and full-day experiences.
*   **The Sanctuary:** About the space, the team, and our philosophy.
*   **Shop:** Retail products and digital gift cards.
*   **Contact:** Location, hours, and inquiry form.

**Key User Flows:**
1.  **The Direct Booking Flow:** Home -> Services -> Specific Treatment Page -> 'Book Now' -> Integration with Booking Software -> Confirmation.
2.  **The Gift Flow:** Home -> Gift Cards -> Selection of Amount/Service -> Personalization -> Checkout -> PDF Email Delivery.
3.  **The Educational Flow:** Social Media -> Blog Post (e.g., 'Benefits of Hydrafacials') -> Related Service Page -> Booking.

**Page-Level Notes:**
Each service page will utilize a 'Sticky' booking bar that remains visible as the user scrolls. The 'Our Team' page will focus heavily on credentials and 'Years of Experience' to build immediate trust in the expertise of the practitioners.
