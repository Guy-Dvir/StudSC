## Strategic Business Objectives

**Primary Business Goals:** The fundamental objective is to transform the website into a high-converting booking engine. We aim to reduce phone-call reliance by migrating 70% of appointment scheduling to the digital platform. Additionally, we seek to drive high-margin revenue through the integrated sale of digital gift cards and premium skincare products.

**Target Audience Breakdown:**
1.  **The Stressed Professional (30-50):** Looking for efficiency, evening/weekend slots, and deep-tissue relief. They value a seamless mobile booking experience.
2.  **The Luxury Seeker (25-65):** Focused on aesthetic results (facials, peels) and the 'experience.' They are influenced by high-quality imagery and social proof.
3.  **The Gift Giver:** Quick-hit visitors looking for a premium gift card with instant delivery options.

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**
-   **Booking Conversion Rate:** Goal of 5% of unique visitors.
-   **Average Order Value (AOV):** Increasing spend through automated add-ons (e.g., aromatherapy upgrades) during the checkout flow.
-   **Retention Rate:** Tracking return bookings through the user account portal.

**Project Constraints:** The site must be fully ADA-compliant and optimized for mobile-first usage, as 80% of spa traffic typically originates from mobile social media referrals.
