## Sitemap Structure

**Main Navigation Structure:**
*   Home
*   Services
*   About
*   Gift Certificates
*   Promotions
*   Contact
*   Book Now (prominently displayed CTA)

**Full Page List with Hierarchy:**
*   **Home**
    *   _Notes:_ Hero image/video, featured services, latest promotions, testimonials, clear Call-to-Action for 'Book Now'.
*   **Services (Parent Page)**
    *   _Notes:_ Overview of categories, compelling imagery, links to sub-pages.
    *   **Massage Therapy**
        *   _Notes:_ Deep tissue, Swedish, hot stone, aromatherapy descriptions, durations, pricing, benefits.
    *   **Facials & Skincare**
        *   _Notes:_ Custom facials, anti-aging, hydra-facials, product lines used, detailed steps.
    *   **Body Treatments**
        *   _Notes:_ Wraps, scrubs, exfoliation, detox treatments, specific product information.
    *   **Nail Care**
        *   _Notes:_ Manicures, pedicures, gel treatments, luxury add-ons.
    *   **Hydrotherapy / Sauna**
        *   _Notes:_ Features, benefits, usage instructions, pricing for access.
    *   **Packages / Rituals**
        *   _Notes:_ Curated multi-service packages, couples' experiences, seasonal rituals, pricing, gift eligibility.
*   **About Us (Parent Page)**
    *   **Our Story / Philosophy**
        *   _Notes:_ Spa history, mission, values, commitment to wellness.
    *   **Meet the Team / Therapists**
        *   _Notes:_ Professional bios, specialties, photos of key staff.
    *   **Location & Contact**
        *   _Notes:_ Detailed directions, map integration, parking info, hours of operation.
*   **Gift Certificates**
    *   _Notes:_ Options for monetary value or specific services/packages, purchase process, delivery options.
*   **Promotions / Specials**
    *   _Notes:_ Seasonal offers, loyalty programs, clear terms and conditions.
*   **Blog / Wellness Tips**
    *   _Notes:_ Articles on self-care, benefits of treatments, healthy lifestyle, product spotlights.
*   **Contact Us**
    *   _Notes:_ Inquiry form, phone, email, social media links, FAQs for common questions.
*   **Book Now (Dedicated Booking Portal)**
    *   _Notes:_ Integration with booking system, step-by-step appointment scheduling.
*   **Legal Pages:** Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Accessibility Statement.

**Key User Flows:**
1.  **New Client Booking Flow:** User lands on Home -> Navigates to Services -> Selects 'Massage Therapy' -> Browses options -> Clicks 'Book Now' on selected service -> Chooses date/time -> Enters client details -> Payment confirmation.
2.  **Gift Certificate Purchase Flow:** User lands on Home -> Clicks 'Gift Certificates' in nav -> Selects value/service -> Adds to cart -> Proceeds to checkout -> Enters recipient/sender info -> Completes payment.
3.  **Information Seeker Flow:** User lands on Home -> Navigates to About Us -> Reads 'Our Story' -> Clicks 'Meet the Team' -> Reviews 'Location & Contact' for directions and hours.
