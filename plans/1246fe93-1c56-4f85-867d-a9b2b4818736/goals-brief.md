## Goals Brief

**Business Objectives:**
*   Increase overall spa revenue by 20% in the next 12 months.
*   Expand client base by attracting new, high-value customers seeking premium wellness experiences.
*   Enhance brand reputation as the leading luxury spa in the region.
*   Improve operational efficiency through streamlined online booking and information dissemination.

**Primary Goals for the Website:**
*   **Drive Online Bookings:** Maximize conversion rates for service bookings and package purchases directly through the website.
*   **Showcase Service Offerings:** Provide comprehensive, appealing details about all treatments, therapies, and packages.
*   **Establish Brand Authority & Trust:** Position the spa as an expert in wellness, demonstrating professionalism and quality.

**Secondary Goals for the Website:**
*   Generate qualified leads for corporate wellness programs or special events.
*   Increase gift certificate sales, particularly during holidays.
*   Grow the email subscriber list for promotions and newsletters.
*   Reduce inbound phone inquiries for basic information.

**Target Audience Breakdown:**
*   **Primary:** Affluent women and men (30-65 years old) living within a 20-mile radius, seeking relaxation, stress relief, and high-quality self-care. They value luxury, service, and professional expertise.
*   **Secondary:** Corporate clients for wellness packages, tourists seeking luxury experiences, and gift-givers (all ages) looking for memorable presents.

**Success Metrics (KPIs):**
*   **Conversion Rate:** Online booking completions (target 5-8%).
*   **Website Traffic:** Unique visitors, particularly from local search (target 15% month-over-month growth).
*   **Average Time on Page:** Especially for service detail pages (target 2-3 minutes).
*   **Bounce Rate:** Overall website (target below 35%).
*   **Gift Certificate Sales:** Monthly revenue tracking.
*   **Email Sign-ups:** Monthly subscriber growth.

**Project Constraints:**
*   **Budget:** Mid-to-high range for a premium, custom-feel website.
*   **Timeline:** 10-12 weeks for initial launch.
*   **Content:** Existing professional photography is limited; significant new photography and videography will be required.
