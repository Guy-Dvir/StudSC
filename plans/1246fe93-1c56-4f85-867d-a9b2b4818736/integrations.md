## Integrations

**Essential Third-Party Tools:**
*   **Online Booking System:** Crucial for seamless client experience and operational efficiency. Must integrate with staff calendars, allow for service selection, time slots, and therapist preferences. Examples: **Mindbody**, **Vagaro**, or **Booksy**. This is non-negotiable for a modern spa.
*   **Payment Gateway:** Secure and reliable processing for all online transactions (bookings, gift certificates, product sales). Must support major credit cards and potentially digital wallets. Examples: **Stripe**, **Square**, or **PayPal**. Essential for conversions.
*   **Website Analytics:** To track user behavior, traffic sources, conversion rates, and overall website performance. **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)** is the standard and absolutely essential for data-driven decisions.
*   **Contact Form Module:** For general inquiries, feedback, and specific requests. Can be a built-in CMS feature or a robust third-party solution like **JotForm** or **Typeform** for advanced conditional logic. Essential for lead capture and customer service.
*   **Email Marketing Platform:** For sending newsletters, promotions, booking confirmations, and post-visit follow-ups. Needs segmentation capabilities. Examples: **Mailchimp**, **Klaviyo**, or **Constant Contact**. Essential for retention and marketing.

**Nice-to-Have Integrations:**
*   **Customer Relationship Management (CRM):** To track client preferences, past services, special notes, and birthday reminders for a highly personalized experience. Could be integrated with the booking system or a standalone like **HubSpot CRM (Free/Starter)**. Useful for building loyalty.
*   **Live Chat Support:** For instant customer service, answering quick questions, and guiding users through the booking process. Examples: **Intercom**, **Tidio**, or **Zendesk Chat**. Enhances user experience and reduces abandonment.
*   **Social Media Feeds:** An embedded Instagram gallery to showcase the spa's aesthetic, daily activities, and promotions visually. Improves engagement and trust.
*   **Review Management Platform:** Displaying client testimonials directly from platforms like **Google My Business**, **Yelp**, or a dedicated review platform. Builds social proof and trust.
*   **Gift Card Sales Platform:** If not fully integrated with the booking system, a dedicated platform like **Gift Up!** can manage digital gift certificate sales and redemption.
*   **Content Delivery Network (CDN):** For faster loading times and improved performance globally, especially with high-resolution imagery and video. (e.g., Cloudflare).
*   **Accessibility Widget:** To ensure ADA compliance and improve user experience for all visitors. (e.g., UserWay, AccessiBe).
