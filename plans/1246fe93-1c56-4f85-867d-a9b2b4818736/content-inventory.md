## Content Inventory

**Copy Sections (Needs to be Created/Refined):**
*   **Home Page:** Compelling headline, engaging introductory paragraph, concise service highlights (3-5), rotating testimonials, strong calls-to-action (e.g., 'Book Now', 'View Services', 'Purchase Gift Card').
*   **Service Pages (Individual):** Unique, detailed descriptions for each service covering benefits, process, duration, pricing structure, key ingredients/products used, and specific therapist expertise (if applicable).
*   **Packages/Rituals:** Benefit-driven descriptions for each package, outlining the sequence of treatments, included amenities, total duration, and value proposition.
*   **About Us (Our Story/Philosophy):** Spa's founding story, mission statement, core values, and unique selling proposition. Therapist bios with qualifications and specialties.
*   **Contact Page:** Clear instructions for reaching the spa (phone, email, inquiry form), detailed address, parking information, and a brief FAQ section covering common queries like cancellation policy or arrival times.
*   **Promotions Page:** Catchy headlines for each offer, clear terms & conditions, expiration dates, and how to redeem.
*   **Blog/Wellness Tips:** 5-10 initial articles covering spa benefits, wellness trends, skincare tips, etc. (e.g., 'Benefits of Deep Tissue Massage', 'Choosing the Right Facial for Your Skin').
*   **Legal Pages:** Comprehensive Privacy Policy and Terms of Service documents.

**Image Types Needed (High-Resolution Professional Photography):**
*   **Hero Images:** Stunning, inviting shots of the spa interior, treatment rooms, relaxation areas, and serene natural elements (water features, botanical arrangements).
*   **Service-Specific Imagery:** Tasteful, professional photos depicting various treatments (e.g., hands-on massage, facial application, nail care), product close-ups, and setup of treatment rooms.
*   **Team Photos:** Professional headshots and a group photo of the spa's therapists and staff.
*   **Atmosphere Shots:** Detail shots capturing the spa's ambiance – candles, warm towels, organic textures, tea ceremony setup, reception area.
*   **Before/After (Conditional):** If applicable and ethically sourced (with client consent) for specific advanced skincare treatments.

**Existing Assets to Collect:**
*   Current logo files (vector and high-res raster).
*   Any existing professional photography or videography (to be reviewed for quality and brand alignment).
*   Any existing brand guidelines, color codes, or font preferences.
*   Testimonials or customer reviews (with permission to publish).
*   List of all services, their durations, and current pricing.
*   Spa hours of operation and contact details.

**Video/Media Needs:**
*   **Introductory Spa Tour Video:** A short (60-90 second) cinematic video showcasing the spa's facilities, ambiance, and key areas.
*   **Service Highlight Videos:** Brief (15-30 second) clips demonstrating aspects of popular services or products in action.
*   **Testimonial Videos:** Short interviews with satisfied clients sharing their experiences (optional, but highly impactful).
