## Brand Personality & Tone
FUNN is built on the pillars of **unfiltered joy** and **seamless connectivity**. The brand personality is high-energy, disruptive, and unapologetically bold, aiming to bridge the gap between digital interaction and real-world excitement. The **Tone of Voice** is punchy and conversational; it avoids corporate jargon in favor of enthusiastic, "hype-focused" microcopy. We speak to the user like a well-connected friend—informative, energetic, and always inviting them to the next big thing.

## Color Palette
The palette utilizes high-contrast vibrance to evoke a sense of movement and "nightlife tech" energy.
*   **Electric Indigo (#6366F1):** The primary brand color, used for core UI elements and brand recognition.
*   **Cyber Pink (#FF007A):** A high-visibility accent color for primary CTAs and "Live" indicators.
*   **Lemon Fizz (#DFFF00):** A disruptive highlight color for secondary buttons and interactive icons.
*   **Deep Space (#0F172A):** The foundational background color to allow vibrant accents to pop.
*   **Cloud White (#F8FAFC):** Used for clean, legible body text and high-contrast negative space.

## Typography Direction
*   **Headings:** **Montserrat (Extra Bold)**. A geometric sans-serif that feels sturdy yet playful. We will utilize tight letter-spacing and all-caps styling for primary headers to command attention.
*   **Body Text:** **Inter (Regular/Medium)**. A highly legible, modern typeface designed for digital screens, ensuring ease of navigation through event details and social feeds.

## Visual Style References
The visual language leans into **"Digital Maximalism"** to create an immersive experience.
*   **Gradients:** Heavy use of mesh gradients blending Indigo and Pink to create depth and movement.
*   **Glassmorphism:** Translucent, frosted-glass overlays for cards and navigation bars to maintain a premium, modern tech aesthetic.
*   **Dynamic Motion:** Subtle entrance animations and high-reactivity hover states to make the interface feel "alive" and responsive to user input.
