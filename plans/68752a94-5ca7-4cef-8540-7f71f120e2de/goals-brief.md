## Goals Brief: Elevating the Spa Experience Online

**Business Objectives:**
*   Significantly increase online bookings for spa services.
*   Expand brand awareness and establish the spa as a premier wellness destination in the region.
*   Showcase the full range of luxurious services and expert therapists.
*   Improve client retention through a seamless online experience and valuable content.
*   Drive sales of gift certificates and potentially retail products.

**Primary Goals for the Website:**
1.  **Maximize Online Booking Conversions:** The website's primary function is to facilitate direct, hassle-free bookings for all services.
2.  **Generate Leads:** Capture email subscribers for newsletters, promotions, and special events.

**Secondary Goals for the Website:**
1.  **Inform and Educate:** Provide comprehensive details about services, benefits, and spa philosophy.
2.  **Enhance Client Loyalty:** Offer an easy way for existing clients to rebook and explore new services.
3.  **Recruit Talent:** Create a professional platform for potential therapists to learn about career opportunities.

**Target Audience Breakdown:**
*   **Primary:** Women aged 30-65 seeking self-care, relaxation, and premium wellness experiences; individuals prioritizing health and beauty.
*   **Secondary:** Couples looking for shared spa experiences; gift-givers purchasing experiences for loved ones; corporate clients interested in wellness packages; tourists seeking local luxury amenities.

**Success Metrics / KPIs:**
*   Online booking conversion rate (percentage of visitors who book).
*   Website traffic (unique visitors, page views).
*   Average time on page (especially service pages).
*   Bounce rate (overall and per key landing page).
*   Email newsletter sign-up rate.
*   Gift certificate sales volume.
*   Engagement with promotions/specials.

**Project Constraints:**
*   **Budget:** Mid-to-high range, allowing for premium design, robust booking integration, and professional content creation.
*   **Timeline:** Approximately 3-4 months for design, development, content population, and launch.
*   **Existing Assets:** Logo and basic brand guidelines exist, but new professional photography and copy will be required.
