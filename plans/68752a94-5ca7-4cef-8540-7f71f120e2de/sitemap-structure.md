## Sitemap Structure: Intuitive Path to Relaxation

**Full Page List with Hierarchy:**
*   **Home** (Landing page, hero banner, featured services, testimonials, prominent 'Book Now' CTA)
*   **Services** (Parent Page - Overview of categories, leads to detailed service pages)
    *   **Massages** (Deep tissue, Swedish, hot stone, aromatherapy, couples massage)
    *   **Facials** (Anti-aging, hydrating, clarifying, men's facial, custom treatments)
    *   **Body Treatments** (Scrubs, wraps, detox, hydrotherapy)
    *   **Nail Care** (Manicures, pedicures, gel treatments)
    *   **Spa Packages** (Curated bundles for various occasions/needs)
    *   **Gift Certificates** (Purchase options, redemption info)
*   **About Us** (Parent Page - Our story, philosophy, mission)
    *   **Our Philosophy** (Brand values, approach to wellness)
    *   **Meet the Team** (Professional bios and photos of therapists)
    *   **Spa Amenities** (Description of facilities, relaxation areas, changing rooms)
*   **Promotions & Specials** (Current offers, seasonal packages)
*   **Products** (If selling retail - skincare, body care, wellness items)
*   **Wellness Blog** (Articles on skincare, mindfulness, spa benefits, health tips)
*   **Contact Us** (Location, map, hours, parking, contact form, phone)
*   **FAQs** (Common questions about bookings, services, preparation, policies)
*   **Careers** (Job openings, application process)
*   **Privacy Policy**
*   **Terms & Conditions**

**Main Navigation Structure:**
*   Home
*   Services (Dropdown/Mega Menu to categories)
*   About Us
*   Promotions
*   Products (if applicable)
*   Wellness Blog
*   Contact Us
*   **'Book Now' (Prominent Call-to-Action button, top right)**

**Key User Flows:**
1.  **Discover & Book Service:** User lands on **Home** > Clicks **Services** > Selects a **Specific Service Page** (e.g., 'Massages') > Reads details and pricing > Clicks **'Book Now'** button > Navigates to external booking system to select date/time and complete booking.
2.  **Purchase Gift Certificate:** User lands on **Home** > Clicks **Services** > Selects **'Gift Certificates'** page > Chooses value/package > Proceeds to secure payment gateway for purchase.
3.  **Find Information & Contact:** User lands on **Home** > Clicks **Contact Us** > Views map, hours, and contact details > Fills out **Contact Form** for inquiries.

**Page-Level Notes:**
*   **Home:** Dynamic hero video/image, concise service highlights, client testimonials slider, clear CTAs.
*   **Service Pages:** Rich descriptions, high-quality imagery, pricing tables, duration, benefits, preparation/aftercare notes, direct booking links for each service.
*   **About Us:** Engaging narrative, professional staff photos, virtual tour potential for 'Spa Amenities'.
*   **Contact Us:** Embed Google Map, detailed parking instructions, clear opening hours, and an accessible contact form.
