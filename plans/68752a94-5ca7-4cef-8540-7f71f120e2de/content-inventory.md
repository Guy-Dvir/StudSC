## Content Inventory: Curating the Spa Experience

**Copy Sections Per Page (Needs to be Created/Written):**
*   **Home:** Catchy tagline, concise introductory paragraph, engaging headlines for featured services, short testimonials, clear Call-to-Action (CTA) text (e.g., 'Book Your Escape', 'Explore Services').
*   **Service Pages (Massages, Facials, etc.):** Detailed descriptions of each service, including benefits, duration, pricing, ideal candidates, preparation instructions, and aftercare advice. Focus on the sensory experience and desired outcomes.
*   **Spa Packages:** Engaging descriptions of each package, highlighting included services, overall value, and target occasions (e.g., 'Couples Retreat', 'Bridal Bliss').
*   **About Us / Our Philosophy:** Compelling narrative about the spa's origins, mission, values, and unique approach to wellness. Emphasize the commitment to client well-being and expertise.
*   **Meet the Team:** Professional bios for each therapist, highlighting their specialties, experience, and passion for their craft. This builds trust and personalization.
*   **Spa Amenities:** Descriptive text about the facilities, relaxation areas, changing rooms, and any unique features (e.g., sauna, steam room, tea lounge).
*   **Promotions & Specials:** Clear, concise descriptions of current offers, including terms and conditions, and expiration dates.
*   **Product Pages (if applicable):** Detailed product descriptions, key ingredients, benefits, usage instructions, and customer reviews.
*   **Wellness Blog:** A minimum of 5-10 initial blog posts on relevant topics like skincare tips, mindfulness, benefits of specific treatments, seasonal wellness, etc.
*   **Contact Us:** Clear address, phone numbers, email, opening hours, detailed parking instructions, and a friendly introductory message for the contact form.
*   **FAQs:** Comprehensive list of common questions regarding bookings, cancellations, arrival times, specific service queries, gift certificates, and accessibility.
*   **Legal Pages:** Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.

**Image Types Needed (High-Resolution & Professional):**
*   **Hero Imagery/Video:** Stunning, high-impact visuals (photos or short video clips) of serene spa environments or tastefully shot service moments.
*   **Spa Interior/Exterior:** Photos showcasing the ambiance, reception, treatment rooms, relaxation areas, and façade.
*   **Service-Specific Imagery:** Artistic and tasteful photos depicting massages, facials, nail care, and body treatments (without explicit nudity, focusing on tranquility and professionalism).
*   **Staff Headshots:** Professional, consistent headshots of all therapists and key staff members.
*   **Product Photography:** Clean, well-lit product shots (if selling retail).
*   **Lifestyle Imagery:** Stock or custom photos depicting relaxation, well-being, and subtle luxury (e.g., a hand receiving a massage, serene nature scenes).

**Existing Assets to Collect:**
*   High-resolution vector logo files.
*   Any existing brand guidelines (colors, fonts).
*   Current service menus and price lists.
*   Existing client testimonials (with permission).
*   Any pre-existing professional photography or video assets.
*   Staff resumes/bios for content creation.

**Video/Media Needs:**
*   **Homepage Hero Video:** Short, atmospheric video showcasing the spa's environment and a glimpse of the experience (30-60 seconds).
*   **Service Highlight Videos (optional):** Very short (15-30 second) clips for 2-3 key services, demonstrating technique or ambiance.
*   **Virtual Tour:** A 360-degree virtual tour of the spa facilities (nice-to-have, but highly impactful).
