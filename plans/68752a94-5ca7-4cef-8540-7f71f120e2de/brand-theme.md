## Brand Theme: Tranquil Luxury

**Color Palette Suggestions:**
*   **Primary:** #5A7E7A (Deep Sage Green) - Evokes nature, calm, and healing.
*   **Secondary:** #BFB39E (Warm Taupe) - Offers grounding, warmth, and sophistication.
*   **Accent 1:** #E0C79F (Muted Gold) - Adds a touch of understated luxury and richness.
*   **Accent 2:** #F0F2F5 (Soft Off-White) - Provides a clean, airy, and spacious feel.
*   **Dark Contrast:** #2E4057 (Deep Navy/Slate) - For strong headings and important text, maintaining elegance.

**Typography Direction:**
*   **Headings:** A sophisticated, elegant serif font like 'Playfair Display' or 'Lora' to convey luxury and timelessness. Ensure good legibility at larger sizes.
*   **Body Text:** A clean, highly readable sans-serif font such as 'Lato' or 'Open Sans' for clarity and a modern feel, ensuring comfort for extended reading.
*   **Subheadings/Accents:** A slightly more decorative but still legible script or thin sans-serif could be used sparingly for visual interest (e.g., 'Josefin Sans Thin').

**Brand Personality:**
Tranquil, luxurious, rejuvenating, professional, serene, and nurturing. The brand should feel like a sanctuary, offering an escape from daily stress and promoting holistic well-being.

**Tone of Voice:**
Serene, inviting, expert, warm, and reassuring. Content should be informative yet soothing, using language that promotes relaxation and trust. Avoid overly clinical or overly casual tones. Emphasize benefits and the experience.

**Visual References:**
Nature-inspired elements (water, soft botanicals, natural textures like wood and stone), minimalist design, high-end hotel spa aesthetics, and wellness retreat imagery. Focus on soft lighting, spaciousness, and organic forms.
