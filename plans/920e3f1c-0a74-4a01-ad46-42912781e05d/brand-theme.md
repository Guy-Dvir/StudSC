## Brand Personality
**HELLO** is defined by its approachability and radical clarity. Positioning itself as the "digital front door" for modern professional networking, the brand is high-energy, inclusive, and forward-thinking. It deliberately avoids the sterile coldness of traditional SaaS, opting instead for a human-centric identity that feels both premium and peer-to-peer.

## Color Palette
*   **Electric Indigo (#4F46E5):** The primary brand color, used for CTA buttons and logos to signify trust and modern connectivity.
*   **Solar Yellow (#FDE047):** An accent color used to inject warmth, optimism, and a literal "sunshine" greeting to the user interface.
*   **Slate Grey (#1E293B):** Used for primary typography and deep-contrast structural elements.
*   **Cloud White (#F8FAFC):** The foundational background color to ensure a clean, breathable, and "airy" user experience.
*   **Soft Mint (#D1FAE5):** A secondary accent used for success states and background shapes to soften the visual flow.

## Typography Direction
*   **Headings:** Use **Plus Jakarta Sans** (Extra Bold). This geometric sans-serif offers a modern, friendly, yet highly professional aesthetic.
*   **Body Text:** Use **Inter** (Regular/Medium). Known for its exceptional readability on screens, it maintains the brand’s "no-nonsense" approach to communication.

## Tone of Voice
The voice is **Empathetic, Direct, and Encouraging.** HELLO speaks like a savvy, helpful colleague. We prioritize "plain English" over industry jargon, using active verbs and personal pronouns to foster an immediate sense of belonging and ease of use.

## Visual Style References
*   **Layout:** "Bento-grid" structures that organize diverse content into clean, modular compartments.
*   **Imagery:** Candid, high-quality photography featuring diverse individuals in collaborative settings, avoiding "staged" stock photos.
*   **UI Elements:** Large border radii (16px+) on containers and subtle glassmorphism effects to create a sense of depth and modern sophistication.
