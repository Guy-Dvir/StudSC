## Website Hierarchy
* **Home**: Immersive hero video, value proposition, and "Start Your Recovery" CTA.
* **Treatments**: Category overview of all modalities.
    * **Whole Body Cryotherapy**: Benefits, safety protocols, and "What to Expect."
    * **Localized Cryo & Facials**: Targeted recovery and aesthetic services.
    * **Red Light Therapy**: Science-backed cellular health benefits.
    * **Compression Therapy**: Lymphatic drainage and circulation focus.
* **Pricing & Memberships**: Comparison table for "Cool Club" tiers vs. single-session drop-ins.
* **Locations**: Dynamic studio finder with map integration and specific studio schedules.
* **The Science**: Educational hub featuring clinical studies and recovery FAQs.
* **Book Now**: Integrated third-party booking engine (e.g., Mindbody or Mariana Tek).

## Navigation Structure
* **Primary Navigation**: Treatments, Memberships, Locations, Science, **[Book Now]** (High-contrast button).
* **Utility Navigation**: Member Login, Gift Cards, Cart.
* **Footer**: Newsletter signup, Social links, Careers, Privacy Policy, Terms of Service.

## Key User Flows
1. **The New Client Conversion**: Home → Treatments (Cryotherapy) → Pricing → Account Creation/Booking.
2. **The High-Intent Member**: Location Search → Select Local Studio → Schedule Session → Quick-Book Confirmation.
3. **The Educational Lead**: Social Media Ad → "The Science" Blog Post → Newsletter Opt-in/Introductory Offer.

## Page-Level Notes
* **Interactive Elements**: Include a "Treatment Finder" quiz on the homepage to recommend services based on user goals (e.g., "Reduce Inflammation" or "Skin Glow").
* **Social Proof**: High-density placement of Google Review widgets and athlete testimonials on all Treatment pages.
* **Mobile Optimization**: Sticky "Book Now" footer bar on all mobile views to minimize friction.
