## Business Objectives
The primary objective for **COOL** is to disrupt the premium functional beverage market by establishing a high-end, direct-to-consumer (DTC) presence. We aim to transition from boutique retail reliance to a digital-first model that prioritizes brand loyalty and high-margin recurring subscription revenue.

## Primary & Secondary Website Goals
*   **Primary Goal:** Drive conversion through a high-performance e-commerce engine, specifically focusing on "Subscribe & Save" checkout flows.
*   **Secondary Goal:** Build a "cult" brand identity through immersive, editorial-style storytelling and lead generation via an interactive "Lifestyle Flavor" quiz.

## Target Audience Breakdown
*   **The Aesthetic Minimalist:** Design-conscious Gen Z and Millennials (ages 20–35) who prioritize "shelf-worthy" packaging and socially shareable lifestyle products.
*   **The Clean Energy Seeker:** Urban professionals and fitness enthusiasts looking for transparent, plant-based functional ingredients without the "big-batch" corporate feel.

## Success Metrics & KPIs
*   **Conversion Rate:** Achieve a minimum 3.2% site-wide conversion rate within the first 90 days.
*   **Subscription Mix:** Secure 45% of total sales through monthly recurring subscriptions.
*   **Average Order Value (AOV):** Maintain an AOV of $55+ through strategic upsells and bundled "Discovery Kits."

## Project Constraints
*   **Platform:** Must be built on Shopify Plus to leverage existing inventory management integrations.
*   **Timeline:** Strict 8-week development cycle to align with the Q3 "Summer of COOL" launch event.
*   **Mobile-First:** 90% of traffic is anticipated from Instagram/TikTok; the UI must be optimized exclusively for mobile interaction.
