## Business Objectives
The primary objective is to elevate **PetEat** from a functional e-commerce catalog to a premium lifestyle brand. By upgrading the aesthetic appeal, we aim to justify premium price points, increase **Average Order Value (AOV)**, and transition one-time buyers into long-term subscribers through a high-trust visual identity.

## Primary & Secondary Website Goals
*   **Primary:** Create a "prestige" shopping experience using high-resolution lifestyle imagery and a clean, modern UI that simplifies the path to purchase.
*   **Secondary:** Improve the **mobile-first UX** for busy pet owners and implement an intuitive filtering system based on breed size, age, and dietary sensitivities (e.g., grain-free).

## Target Audience Breakdown
*   **The Health-Conscious Pet Parent:** High-income individuals who view their dogs as family members and prioritize nutritional integrity over cost.
*   **The Subscription-Seeker:** Busy urban professionals looking for a "set it and forget it" solution for high-quality pet nutrition.
*   **The Research-Driven Owner:** Customers looking for transparent ingredient lists and local Israeli sourcing to ensure freshness.

## Success Metrics (KPIs)
*   **Conversion Rate:** A 15–20% increase in add-to-cart actions on the Dog Food collection page.
*   **Subscription Growth:** A measurable rise in "Subscribe & Save" enrollments at checkout.
*   **User Engagement:** Reduction in bounce rate by improving page load speeds and visual storytelling.

## Project Constraints
*   **Backend Integration:** Design must work within the existing Shopify/e-commerce infrastructure.
*   **Bilingual Support:** All layouts must accommodate **RTL (Hebrew) and LTR (English)** typography without breaking the "pretty" aesthetic.
*   **Content Dependency:** Success is contingent on high-quality professional photography to replace current stock or low-res assets.
