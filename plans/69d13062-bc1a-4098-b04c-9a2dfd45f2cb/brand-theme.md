## Brand Personality & Tone
*   **Brand Personality:** Nurturing, premium, and energetic. The brand positions itself as a high-end wellness partner for pet parents, balancing scientific credibility with the heartfelt joy of pet ownership. It should feel like a "boutique health food store" for dogs rather than a bulk warehouse.
*   **Tone of Voice:** Warm, expert, and encouraging. The copy should speak directly to the pet’s well-being, using benefit-driven language (e.g., "Fuel their adventure") rather than just listing raw specifications.

## Color Palette
*   **Deep Forest (#2D5A27):** A rich, organic green used for primary branding to evoke nature, health, and trust.
*   **Golden Yolk (#F2AF42):** A vibrant, appetizing yellow-orange for Call-to-Action buttons and "New" badges to create urgency and energy.
*   **Warm Bone (#F9F7F2):** A soft, off-white background color that provides a more premium, "human-grade" feel than stark white.
*   **Earth Charcoal (#373737):** Used for typography to ensure high readability while feeling softer and more natural than pure black.
*   **Eucalyptus (#A3B18A):** A muted secondary green for hover states and icon backgrounds.

## Typography Direction
*   **Primary Headings:** A bold, rounded Sans-Serif (e.g., **Lexend** or **Quicksand**). This communicates friendliness, accessibility, and modern aesthetics.
*   **Body & UI Text:** A clean, high-legibility Sans-Serif (e.g., **Inter** or **Montserrat**). This ensures that complex nutritional information and ingredient lists are easy to scan on mobile devices.

## Visual Style References
*   **Imagery:** High-resolution lifestyle photography of happy dogs in natural outdoor settings and "top-down" ingredient shots that look fresh and gourmet. 
*   **UI Elements:** Large, soft-cornered cards (16px radius), subtle drop shadows to create depth, and generous white space to eliminate the cluttered "grid" feel of the current site. Use custom-drawn line icons for dietary features (e.g., "Grain Free," "High Protein").
